Welcome to the Pig Dice Game!

Common rules are:

  • Roll - Rolls the dice
  • Add - Adds round score to total score
  • Winning Score - You can set the winning score, default is 50

There are 3 variants of this game:

Variant 1: (Simple Pig)

  • 1 dice game
  • Roll a 1, you lose your round score and turn

Variant 2: (Two-Dice Pig)

  • 2 dice game
  • Roll a 1, you lose your round score and turn
  • Roll two 1's, you lose your total score and turn

Variant 3: (Big Pig)

  • 2 dice game
  • Roll a 1, you lose your round score and turn
  • Roll two 1's, you add 25 to your round score
  • Roll other doubles, you add twice the value of the dice to your round score
